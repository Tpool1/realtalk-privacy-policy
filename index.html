Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026
OpenSpeak is a language-learning app. This policy explains what data the current app and backend use, where it is stored, and how it is shared with service providers.

1. Data We Collect

Account and authentication data
OpenSpeak uses Firebase Authentication. Depending on how you sign in, this can include your Firebase user ID (UID), email address, and display name.

Profile and social data
If you use profile, friends, or study-group features, we process:

  • Display name and username
  • Friend code
  • Optional profile photo (if you choose one)
  • Friend requests, friend connections, and study-group membership/invites

Conversation and learning data
To run the AI tutor, we send conversation text and prompt context (for example scenario details, selected language/proficiency, and optional custom-scene inputs such as location or partner). We also generate learning analytics from conversation text (for example corrections, principle labels, and progress signals).

Voice input data
In voice mode, microphone audio is captured for live speech recognition and converted to text. OpenSpeak uses transcript text and speech confidence for the session. OpenSpeak does not upload raw audio to its own backend.

On-device app data
OpenSpeak stores app state locally on your device (UserDefaults), including onboarding selections, preferences, streak/progress counters, and local mastery/error-pattern data used for coaching and progress screens.

Subscription status data
OpenSpeak uses RevenueCat to retrieve offerings, process purchases/restores, and determine whether your premium entitlement is active.

App integrity data
OpenSpeak attaches Firebase App Check tokens to backend requests to verify the app instance.

2. How We Use Data

  • Provide conversations and feedback - We use message text and context to generate tutor responses and linguistic feedback.
  • Operate account and social features - We use account/profile data for sign-in, profile display, friend search/requests, and study-group invites/membership.
  • Track learning progress - We use locally stored progress signals to personalize prompts and show progress insights.
  • Manage subscriptions - We use entitlement and purchase state to unlock premium features.
  • Protect services - We use App Check tokens to reduce abuse of backend endpoints.

We do not sell personal data.

3. Services and Data Sharing

ChatGPT (via OpenSpeak proxy)
Conversation text and prompt context are sent to OpenSpeak's backend proxy, which forwards AI generation requests to ChatGPT (OpenAI).

Google Firebase
We use Firebase Authentication (sign-in), Firebase App Check (request attestation), Cloud Firestore (profiles, friends, study groups/invites), and Firebase Storage (profile photos).

RevenueCat and Apple in-app purchase infrastructure
Subscription purchase and entitlement flows are handled through RevenueCat and Apple's purchase systems.

Apple frameworks
OpenSpeak uses Apple Speech/AVFoundation for voice input and Apple's Translation framework for in-app translations. Processing behavior for these services is governed by Apple.

These providers may process data under their own terms and retention policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI (ChatGPT) Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Privacy and Security
  • RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Apple Privacy Policy

4. Data We Do Not Intentionally Collect

OpenSpeak does not request or use the following for app functionality:

Precise location Contacts Health data Advertising identifiers Financial account numbers

Camera/photo library access is only used if you choose to upload a profile photo.

5. Storage and Retention

On device - Local preferences and learning/progress data are stored on your device until you clear app data or remove the app.

Backend storage - Account-related profile, friends, and study-group records are stored in Firebase services while your account/features remain active.

AI requests - OpenSpeak's proxy forwards generation requests and does not implement long-term conversation storage in app code; infrastructure providers may keep operational logs according to their policies.

Account deletion - In-app account deletion removes your authentication account. If you want additional backend profile/social records removed, contact us at the email below.

6. Your Choices

  • You can edit profile fields and choose whether to upload a profile photo.
  • You can sign out of your account in the app.
  • You can request account deletion in the app.
  • You can disable or stop using voice mode and social features at any time.

7. Children's Privacy

OpenSpeak is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. We will post updates here and revise the "Last updated" date.
