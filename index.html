RealTalk

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026
RealTalk is a conversational language-learning app. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how it is used. We collect only what is necessary to make the app work and never sell your data.

1. Data We Collect

Voice & Speech
When you use Voice Mode, your spoken audio is transcribed locally on your device using Apple's Speech Recognition framework. The resulting text (not the raw audio) is used to drive the conversation. Audio is never recorded or stored by RealTalk.

Conversation Transcripts
The text of your practice conversations — your messages and the AI partner's responses — is sent to our AI service to generate replies and linguistic feedback. See Third-Party Services below for details.

Learning Profile
To personalize your experience, we store the following locally on your device:

  • Target language and proficiency level
  • Life context (e.g., travel, work, relationship) and optional free-text description
  • Learning goals (e.g., speaking confidence, business language)
  • Practice mode preference

Performance & Progress Data
We store locally on your device:

  • Error patterns by linguistic category (verb conjugation, subjunctive mood, etc.)
  • Vocabulary mastery scores
  • Session counts and practice streak dates

Study Group Data
If you join a Study Group, we store:

  • A randomly generated, anonymous learner ID (e.g., learner_1713103200_4821) — no name or email is attached
  • Group codes you have joined
  • Shared practice cards (corrections and example sentences generated by the AI)

The Instructor Dashboard displays aggregated and anonymized error patterns across learners in a group. Individual learner data is not exposed to instructors.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • AI conversation & feedback — Conversation text is sent to our AI service to generate responses and linguistic analysis (grammar corrections, vocabulary suggestions).
  • Personalization — Your learning profile is used entirely on-device to tailor practice scenarios, difficulty, and feedback to your goals.
  • Progress tracking — Mastery and error data are stored locally to show you your improvement over time.
  • Study Groups — Your anonymous learner ID lets you contribute to and benefit from shared group practice materials.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app.

3. Third-Party Services

Google Gemini (via Cloud Run proxy)
Conversation transcripts, your learning profile context, and system prompts are sent to a Google Cloud Run proxy that forwards requests to the Google Gemini AI model. This is used to generate conversation responses and linguistic feedback. Google's data handling is governed by the Google Privacy Policy and the Google Cloud Data Processing Addendum.

Firebase (Google)
We use Firebase App Check to verify that requests to our AI proxy originate from the genuine RealTalk app, preventing abuse. Firebase Cloud Messaging infrastructure is present but not currently used to send push notifications. Firebase is governed by the Firebase Privacy Policy.

Apple Speech Recognition & AVFoundation
Voice transcription is performed on-device using Apple's native frameworks. Apple's handling of speech data is governed by the Apple Privacy Policy.

4. Data We Do Not Collect

RealTalk does not collect or access:

Name or email Location Camera or photos Contacts Health data Advertising identifiers Browsing history Financial information

There are no accounts, no sign-in, and no server-side user profiles.

5. Data Storage & Retention

On-device storage — All learning profile, mastery, and progress data is stored in your device's local storage (UserDefaults). This data never leaves your device except as noted in Section 3. It persists until you delete the app.

Conversation transcripts — Text sent to the AI service for real-time analysis is not stored by RealTalk after the session ends. The AI service (Google Gemini) may retain request data in accordance with its own policies.

Deleting your data — Uninstalling RealTalk from your device permanently deletes all locally stored data.

6. Children's Privacy

RealTalk is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided data through the app, please contact us at the address below.
